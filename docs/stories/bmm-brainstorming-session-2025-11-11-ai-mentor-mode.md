## Brainstorming Topic: AI Mentor Mode

### Main Idea: AI Mentor Mode

An AI-powered mentor that suggests how each new skill aligns with potential job roles, keeping the focus on career impact, and acting as a continuous guide for the user.

### How the "AI Mentor Mode" would function:

**1. "Skill to Job" Mapping:**
*   When the user is learning a new skill, the AI Mentor would provide a dynamic, real-time analysis of how that skill translates to the job market.
*   It would show:
    *   Job roles where the skill is in high demand.
    *   Average salary expectations for those roles.
    *   Potential for career growth.
    *   "Skill synergy" score, showing how new skills combine with existing ones.

**2. "Project to Portfolio" Guidance:**
*   When the user is working on a project, the AI Mentor would guide them on how to best showcase it.
*   Suggestions would include:
    *   How to frame the project on their CV to highlight learned skills.
    *   What to include in their portfolio.
    *   How to discuss the project in an interview.

**3. "Micro-learning" and "Just-in-Time" Advice:**
*   The AI Mentor would provide small, bite-sized pieces of advice and encouragement.
*   Examples:
    *   Suggestions on how to showcase newly acquired skills on a CV.
    *   Relevant articles or resources based on career interests.
    *   Reminders to take breaks and recharge.

**4. "Career Storytelling" Assistance:**
*   The AI Mentor could help the user craft a compelling "career story" that connects their skills, experiences, and aspirations.
*   This would assist in:
    *   Writing engaging cover letters.
    *   Answering interview questions with confidence.
    *   Building a stronger personal brand.

The "AI Mentor Mode" would act as a constant companion and guide, helping users not just acquire new skills, but understand their value and leverage them to achieve career goals.
